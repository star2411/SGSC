What is Gastric Surgery?

Gastric surgery, often referred to as bariatric surgery, is a medical procedure aimed at helping individuals manage obesity and other digestive system issues. At STAR GASTRIC SURGICAL CENTER, we specialize in advanced minimally invasive procedures, including gastric sleeve, gastric bypass, and other weight-loss surgeries. Our team also manages post-surgical care and provides comprehensive support through diagnosis, nutrition, and follow-up treatment.

Our Procedures

We offer state-of-the-art gastric sleeve surgery, gastric bypass, and laparoscopic bariatric techniques, using the latest technology and patient-centered care methods.
